Tank Tips:

  • Keepin' It Clean: Use a proper filter for your tank's type and size. Make sure to change the water regularly, and consider using an algae eater.
  • Keepin' It Regular: Take frequent gravity measurements to keep the salinity level constant. To accomodate for evaporation, keep a supply of premixed saltwater to refill the tank.
  • Keepin' It Warm: Position a heater in the center of your tank to maintain a stable temperature. Make sure the heater is the right type for the tank's size.
